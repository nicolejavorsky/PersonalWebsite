About Me
I'm a student at Barnard College who is passionate about creativity, social change, and innovation. I started an organization called Cubs for Coping, which donates handmade teddy bears to hospitals, homeless shelters and eating disorder programs. I have interned for DoSomething.org, one of the largest organizations for young people and social change. I write for the Columbia Spectator and I love writing. On my free time, I enjoy taking trapeze and dance classes.
